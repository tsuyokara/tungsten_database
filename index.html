Tungsten integration index — ranked success metrics
1
Alignment percentage
Ratio of intelligent control to total asset mass. Measures overlap between owned assets and Tungsten's intelligent integrated systems. Prevents conglomerate bloat — Tungsten will deliberately slow resource capture to protect this metric. Composite: owned+integrated, owned-not-integrated, integrated-not-owned.
2
Resource capture-accumulation-control
Incoming capital + value creation relative to input. Output/input ratio must always expand, not just stay positive. Disproportionate value creation is the standard. Early phase: profit-oriented for rapid scaling.
3
Watt-creation per resource input
Primary owner: Grid. Energy efficiency and scale as a unified metric. Oversees power creation efficiency across all Tungsten systems. Everything Tungsten does is ultimately an energy transformation problem.
